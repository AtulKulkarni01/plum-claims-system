Generated by `python -m scripts.make_samples`. Use these in the **Upload documents**
tab. For each file, pick the matching document type in the dropdown. The decision
is driven by what Gemini reads from the image, so amounts come from the bill.

Notes:
- Amounts/diagnoses are read live by Gemini from the images, so small wording
  differences in the model's reading are normal.
- The fully deterministic versions of all 12 graded cases are in **Test case** mode.
- `DOCUMENT_ALTERATION` and fraud signals are easiest to demo in Test case mode,
  since they depend on specific document content.
